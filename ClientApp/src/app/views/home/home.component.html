What is IJCAI?

International Journal of Accounting and Finance (IJCAI) is peer-reviewed and published

every quarter of the year. IJCAI provides a global forum to enhance communication

among scholars and practitioners on emerging and established areas of accountancy

and finance research specifically and business administration, economics and

operations generally.
